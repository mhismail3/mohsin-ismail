---
title: "Shiproom cadence"
date: "2025-03-04"
tags: ["process", "cadence", "planning"]
summary: "Weekly shiproom focused on small, high-quality releases with clear acceptance criteria."
---

I moved to a weekly shiproom: a short checklist-driven call that greenlights only the smallest, highest-leverage changes. Each item must have:

- A crisp acceptance statement.
- A rollback note.
- A log line added to the public changelog.

The cadence keeps momentum without bloating the backlog.
